ABOUT

Prabha's Reading Corner is a mini lending library and have many interesting collection of books for all age group of readers. Our library consist of books under these categories mentioned below.

  1. Fiction Books

       Fiction Books refers to the type of book or story that is written about imaginary characters and events and does not describe real people or deal with real facts.
    Below are the different categories of Fiction books.
    1. Novels
    2. FairyTales
    3. Horror
    4. Adventure

  2. Non-Fiction Books

       Non-Fiction Books refers to the type of book or story that is written based on True events and facts.
    Below are the different categories of NonFiction books.
    1. Biography
    2. AutoBiography
    3. Encylopedia/Dictonary
    4. Self-Help
